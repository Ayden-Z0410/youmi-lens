## ⚡ Quick Start

1. Download the DMG file  
2. Open the app  
3. Click **Start** to begin recording  
4. View captions in real time  
5. Generate summaries after recording  

---

## 🎯 What it does

• Real-time English captions (Deepgram-powered)  
• Chinese translation support  
• Lecture recording and saving  
• AI-generated summaries (English + Chinese)  

---

## 💡 Why I built this

Lectures can be hard to follow for international students.

Professors speak fast. You miss details. Notes become incomplete.

Youmi Lens is built to fix that.

---

## 👀 Who is this for?

• International students  
• Non-native English speakers  
• Anyone struggling to follow fast-paced lectures
